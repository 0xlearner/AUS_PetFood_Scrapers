    Dog Food

    Our furry friends deserve only the best, and a diet of premium dog food helps guarantee them the best health and daily life. Todays pet nutritionists and vets have created delicious and nutrition focused dog foods that work to boost your dogs health from the outside in. A range of dog medical issues can be managed through diet, from skin issues, to digestion and joint pain. Petbarn is here to help you feed your dog a balanced and complete diet.



    Dry Dog Food

    Dry dog food consists of kibble pellets, dried meats, grains and vegetables. The dehydrated foods are processed into bite size pieces so they can be easily eaten by dogs. The dry nature helps support the dog's digestion and dental health, and it can be fortified to include certain nutrients and minerals.

    Shop Now

    Wet Dog Food

    Wet dog food contains cooked ground meat, grains and veggies that have been processed to keep a high water content. Whilst dry food has been dehydrated and usually combined with meals, wet food instead is canned and once opened, needs to be kept in the fridge. Wet food usually offers a higher protein content and less calories, and doesnt need to be chewed.

    Shop Now

    Raw, Fresh & Frozen Dog Food

    Raw, fresh and frozen food provides your dog with an evolutionary diet appropriate for them. This type of dog food consists of cooked meat, grains and veggies with few to none preservatives with a shorter shelf life than dry food or wet foods. Fresh dog foods tend to be a more nutrient dense meal option for dogs and must be used within 5-7 days.

    Shop Now

    Puppy Food

    Puppy food formulas let your puppy feast on delicious meals whilst receiving all the nutrients they need to grow into a happy and healthy dog. Our puppy food range boasts a range of brands so you can find the diet best suited for your dogs size and breed. A healthy diet is essential to raising a healthy puppy and this wide range of options guarantees something for every lifestyle.

    Shop Now

    Grain Free Dog Food

    Grain free dog food is an ideal choice for dogs with sensitive stomachs. Full of proteins, vitamins, vegetables and lower carbohydrates, grain free dog food can be a nutritious alternative for your dog. Always consult a vet before switching dog foods to find out whats best for your pet and their breed.

    What is the best food for dogs?

    The best food for dogs is vet approved fresh, dry or wet foods that have been formulated with the pet's health needs in mind. The best food for your dog will always depend upon their age, breed and lifestyle, but in general, following a vet recommended diet will help maintain and manage your pets health and wellbeing long term.

    Petbarns collection of dog foods stocks only high quality and pet-safe food options. Just like with our diets, dogs benefit from quality ingredients, nutrient-dense and balanced diets that've been personalised to them. If youre not sure what food is best for your dog, try Petbarns Dog Food Finder.

    Although known for their appetites, dogs have sensitive stomachs and intolerances to specific foods and require a tailored and pet-safe diet thats separate from their pet parent.
